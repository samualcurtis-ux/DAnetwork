Key Notes:
• Wetterhorn Wireless L.L.C. is a wholly-owned subsidiary of DISH Network / EchoStar Corporation. Post Jan 2024 DISH-EchoStar merger, CBRS PALs were transferred to EchoStar Wireless Holding L.L.C.
• Spectrum Wireless Holdings, LLC is Charter Communications' auction bidding entity.
• All PALs are county-wide, 10-year term (issued ~2021, expiring ~2031), renewable. 47 CFR Part 96.
• EchoStar is actively divesting spectrum as of 2025-2026. DISH CBRS PALs status may change; monitor FCC ULS for transfers.
• GAA (General Authorized Access) tier remains available in all 3 counties on any unused PAL channels + 3650-3700 MHz band (50 MHz).
• SoCal Edison's PAL acquisition reflects utility private network strategy for grid infrastructure.
